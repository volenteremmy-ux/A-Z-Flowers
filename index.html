Osteospermum

African Daisy

Vibrant, daisy-like flowers native to southern Africa, known for their bold colors like purple, orange, and white. They bring a unique beauty to gardens, thriving in full sun and symbolizing cheerfulness and strength.

Centaurea cyanus

Bachelor's Button

Also known as the cornflower, this charming bloom is famous for its vivid blue petals. With its delicate, papery texture, it adds a touch of wild beauty to meadows, symbolizing hope and devotion.

Dianthus caryophyllus

Carnations

Elegant, ruffled blooms known for their spicy-sweet fragrance. Available in a wide range of colors, they are often used to express love, admiration, and remembrance.
